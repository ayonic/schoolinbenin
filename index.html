HOW TO APPLY AS A FRESHMAN, A TRANSFER, OR A DIRECT ENTRY STUDENT

HIGH / SECONDARY SCHOOL (FRESHMAN)

Minimum of five O Level (WAEC, NECO, GCE, NABTEB or equivalent) Credit passes at not more than two(2) sittings to include English language, Mathematics, other two other sciences(Chemistry & Physics), social sciences or Arts subjects are required for admission into Faculty of Communication Studies and social sciences.
