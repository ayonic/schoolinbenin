About Us

INSTITUTE UNIVERSITAIRE ULTIME is a private university of Management and Technology, situated in Benin Republic. IUU University was founded and established in 2002 by Hon Dr Kunle Omobomi, Founder, and holder of the Higher Education Diploma. IUU was accredited the same year to offer certificates, diplomas, undergraduate degrees, and postgraduate programs by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research (MHESR) in the Republic of Benin. Our core aim is to train senior managers in the fields of Management and Technology, transform academic knowledge into practical solutions and in all forms of initiatives leading to self-actualization, become a world-class institution for academic excellence, expand the frontiers of knowledge through the provision of excellent academic environment for learning and research works.

IUU is a bilingual university open to French and English-speaking students. We offer conducive learning environments, quality resources, and well-trained instructors whose duty is to equip you with the right knowledge and training you need for your career path.

At IUU University, we strive on a daily to become one of the best universities in this 21st century by ensuring the quality of our programs through careful focusing of our resources in areas of institutional strength. Our University is well-recognized for the quality of its undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional programs, and all our courses are well-accredited at IUU University.

IUU is a great citadel of learning focused on academic excellence. The IUU Benin Republic is an established and enlightened higher institute of learning that has been verified and certified by the educational boards to award Bachelor’s and master’s degrees to deserving students. We are also accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, Republic of Benin. Situated at Poissonnerie Agongon, Seme-podji Carrefour. Our training is based on a pedagogical approach alternating teaching and internship. We are located in Davie, Porto-Novo, Republique du Benin.

 

DISTANCE  LEARNING

Study with IUU University from anywhere in the world

The Distance Learning Centre, IUU University is a unique Distance Learning Centre of repute, excelling in the delivery of certificates, diplomas, and undergraduate and postgraduate programs in all fields. Our Distance Learning Center is designed to provide the ability to obtain degrees in the school no matter where you live. The program delivers the knowledge and skills you need to advance your career whenever and wherever it’s convenient for you.

Students from different walks of life are choosing to enroll at the Distance Learning Centre, IUU University in record numbers and for many reasons but they all share the same goal: to earn a high-quality education. The DLC gives students the best of both worlds as they get to learn anywhere, anytime, and anyhow from some of the best and brightest in their fields, as our e-learning courses are taught by the same renowned faculty members who teach in our on-campus programs.

Why Study At IUU University?

The distance learning program at IUU University is centered on using ICT resources to coach, instruct, and promote effective learning among students in different locations. The education system at IUU University is accessible to everyone regardless of age, gender, place of employment, race, religion, or distance, among other factors. We offer facilities, study materials, and online learning tools for active learning.

 

HOW TO APPLY AS A FRESHMAN, A TRANSFER, OR A DIRECT ENTRY STUDENT

HIGH / SECONDARY SCHOOL (FRESHMAN)

Minimum of five O’ Level (WAEC, NECO, GCE, NABTEB or equivalent) Credit passes at not more than two(2) sittings to include English language, Mathematics, other two other sciences(Chemistry & Physics), social sciences or Arts subjects are required for admission into Faculty of Communication Studies and social sciences.
